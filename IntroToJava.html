What is Computer Science?

Most people think computer science is all about code, however that isn't true. Computer Science involves problem solving, and that skill can not only lead one to jobs, but can also create an interest in learning the profession. Problem solving is the most efficient way of expressing your idea, but to do that you need an algorithm.

What is an algorithm? An algorithm is a set of steps that is used to solve a problem. For example, to make oatmeal, you have to boil the milk then put oats. After that, wait for a while to cool down and then add your favorite toppings. This method of solving problems may seem hard at first, however it will lead you to the right path. This process has helped me program more efficiently even throughout learning the basics of JAVA.
